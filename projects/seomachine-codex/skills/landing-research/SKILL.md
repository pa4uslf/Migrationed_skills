# Landing Research

Research a landing-page opportunity and save the brief for later writing.

## Output

Save to:

`research/landing-brief-<topic-slug>-<YYYY-MM-DD>.md`

## Include

- Target keyword and intent
- SEO vs PPC recommendation
- Competitor messaging patterns
- Headline ideas
- CTA ideas
- Trust signals to include
- Gaps to exploit

## Codex-Specific Notes

- This is primarily a research-and-writing skill. There is no dedicated script yet because the inputs are usually external and qualitative.
